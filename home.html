Our Business

HDFC Bank caters to a wide range of banking services
covering commercial investment banking on the wholesale side
and transactional / branch banking on the retail side.
The bank has three key business segments

Treasury

Within this business, the bank has three main product areas
Foreign Exchange and Derivatives, Local Currency Money Market & Debt Securities,
and Equities. With the liberalisation of the financial markets in India.

Retail Banking

The objective of the Retail Bank is to provide its target market customers
a full range of financial products and banking services,
giving the customer a one-stop window for all his/her banking requirements.

Wholesale Banking

The Bank's target market is primarily large, blue-chip manufacturing companies
in the Indian corporate sector and to a lesser extent, small & mid-sized
corporates and agri-based businesses
